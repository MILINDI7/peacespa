Massages

Body to body

This involves close physical contact and the use of body to body techniques such as sliding and rubbing to create a heightened sense of connection and relaxation. It aims to release tension and promote circulation.

Lomi Lomi

This Hawaiian healing technique involves long, flowing strokes using the therapist's hands, forearms and elbows; It aims to release tension , promote relaxation and restore balancein the body and mind.

Back massage

This is a therapeutic treatment focused on the muscles and tissues of the back through various techniques such as kneading, rubbing, and applying pressure. It aims to alleviate tension, reduce muscle stiffneess and promotes relaxation mainly in the back area

Foot

This is a therapeutic practice that involves applying pressure and manipulating the muscles and tissues of the feet. It is based on the belief that specific reflex points on the feet correspond to different areas of the body. By stimulating these points, foot massage aims to promote relaxation, relieve tension, improve blood circulation, and enhance overall well-being.

Scalp & face massage

Scalp and face massage is a therapeutic technique that involves applying gentle pressure and manipulating the muscles and tissues of the scalp and face. It is designed to promote relaxation, relieve tension, and improve overall well-being. This helps to increase blood flow to the scalp, release muscle tension, and stimulate hair follicles.

Antistress candle therapy

Antistress candle therapy is a form of aromatherapy that combines the calming effects of candlelight and soothing fragrances to promote relaxation and reduce stress. As the candles burn, they release the aromatic scents into the air, which can have a calming effect on the mind and body. The soft, flickering candlelight also helps to create a peaceful atmosphere, encouraging relaxation and mindfulness.

Classic aromatherapy

Classic aromatherapy is a holistic healing practice that harnesses the power of aromatic plant extracts, known as essential oils, to promote well-being and enhance health. It involves the use of carefully selected essential oils derived from flowers, herbs, fruits, and other plant parts. In classic aromatherapy, essential oils are used in various ways, such as inhalation, topical application, or through baths. The aromatic molecules of the oils are believed to interact with the body and mind, producing therapeutic effects.

Le signature deep tissue

Le Signature Deep Tissue massage is a specialized massage technique that focuses on targeting deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue in the body. It is designed to release chronic tension and alleviate muscle stiffness and pain. It aims to break down adhesions (knots) and scar tissue in the muscles, improve blood circulation, and promote overall relaxation and well-being.

Sport massage

Sport massage is a specialized form of massage therapy that is tailored to the needs of athletes and individuals engaged in physical activities. It focuses on enhancing athletic performance, preventing injuries, and promoting faster recovery. he main goals of sport massage include improving flexibility, increasing range of motion, reducing muscle tension, promoting blood circulation, and alleviating muscle soreness. It can also help with injury rehabilitation and enhance overall athletic performance.

Pregnant massage

Pregnancy or prenatal massage is used to reduce stress, reduce swelling in the arms and legs and relieve muscle and joint pain in pregnant women. Massage in pregnancy can involve many different massage techniques. It is usually a gentle massage.

Hot Stone massage

Hot stone therapy is a specialist massage that uses smooth, flat, heated basalt stones that are positioned along your spine, in the palms of your hands, along your legs and between the toes. Typically from river beds, basalt stones are used for massage as they have a rich iron content that retains heat.
